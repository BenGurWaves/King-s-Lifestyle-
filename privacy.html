Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

A King's Lifestyle collects only the information you voluntarily provide. This includes profile details such as your name, preferences for daily routines, and any personal goals or habits you choose to track within the application. We do not collect data passively or without your knowledge.

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This means your information remains on your machine by default and is not transmitted elsewhere. If you choose to create an account and enable cloud sync, your data will be stored securely in our Supabase-hosted database. You may use the application entirely offline with local storage alone.

Cookies & Local Data

We use localStorage to persist your preferences, progress, and application state between sessions. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party cookie-based analytics. The only data stored in your browser is what you directly create through using the application.

Third-Party Services

When cloud sync is enabled, we use Supabase for secure data storage and authentication. Supabase processes data in accordance with their own privacy policy. We use Google Fonts for typography rendering. No other third-party services receive your personal data. We do not sell, trade, or share your information with advertisers or data brokers.

Your Data Rights

You retain full ownership of your data at all times. You may export, modify, or delete your locally stored data by clearing your browser's localStorage. If you have enabled cloud sync, you may request complete deletion of your cloud-stored data by contacting us. We will process all deletion requests promptly and confirm completion.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Local data is secured by your device and browser protections. Cloud-synced data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored in secure, access-controlled databases. No method of electronic storage is completely secure, but we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, data requests, or any concerns regarding your information, please reach out through calyvent.com.
